CUSTOM CABBING 

WE ARE PROUD TO ANNOUNCE A NEW SERVICE FROM TRIPLE A ROCKSHOP

Over the past few months we have had the pleasure of cutting cabs and offering them to several designers that make a living at selling quality jewelry and asking for the best work possible. Since this has been a great success and the orders keep mounting and more designers are coming forth for our work and experience. Plus we have now merged with the employees of the old Tepee Rockshop and are working together to offer you this service. We here at the shop have under one roof, over 96 years of experience in our personnel and staff at performing this service to and for you. We would like to offer the same service to the public and other business's that require mass amounts of cabs made for their business purposes.

We will take your pre-forms, calibrate and dome your cabochons from your material. We can do most sizes from 18x13 to 38x52 ovals and 15mm to 50mm rounds. All we ask is that you mark and snap out or trim saw the cabochon blanks and send them to us. The blanks must have at least 4mm of excess material and no more than 5mm overall as our cabbing machine works well with only that much excess. Anymore than this and we are wasting your valuable material. Then we can either finish them completely by tumble polishing or send them back for you to polish and finish. We do not do any hand polishing! We will require however, that there be at least 100 blanks as a minimum order of any one size. The following price list is based on material blanks that are 3/16 of an inch thickness and if your blanks are 1/4 inch thick, please add ten cents extra to each blank or stone.

There are some stones that we will not do as they are too tricky to tumble polish and there are some stones that we will not touch as for the toxic nature of the stone or shell. The stones that we will not touch are as follows,
Malachite Rose Quartz Mother of Pearl Opal Onyx

And any of the more toxic materials that are considered radioactive or have a base of heavy metals or metals in general.

There are some stones that you take the chance of having the material pull when tumble polishing and they are,
Tiger Iron Tiger Eye Some plumes Rutilated Quartz Jade

CUSTOM SLABING

We can and will do custom slabing for our clients as to do the slabs and prepare them for marking out the cabs or just slabing the material for your own personal use. We have several saws on the line and the people to man the saws, as we have saws from 12inch too 24 inch saws. We cannot however read the peoples mind as to how they would like the material slabbed, so we take the easiest way and slab from there and we slab it all. For this part of the service we here at the shop charge $.50 per square inch for slabing your material. This does not cover any shipping and handling.
If you want to have us slab and trim the blanks for you as to be cabbed by our machines, we charge $20.00 per hour to trim and prepare the blanks for cabbing. This is over and above the slabbing charge for the stones and all excess material and scraps will be returned to you packaged, unless you want to have us dispose of the scraps. We cannot read our clients minds and for orientations of the stones for the cabbing, we will cab as we see fit and to achieve the most cabs from the slabs that we cut or the slabs that you send unless so instructed.

 

CUSTOM POLISHING SLABS
For years we have been polishing cabs and the like for people and now we offer yet another service to you and that is we will polish slabs to a mirror finish. Just remember that if you put in good material in for polishing, you'll get good slabs out. Send us slabs that are fractured and with cracks, you'll get back pieces rather than slabs. We are asking that a minimum of 30 pounds be the lowest amount that be handled for polishing slabs. The sizes and price per pound are as follows and does not include shipping and handling.

1" - 1 ½" slabs $ 9.00 per pound
1 ½" - 4" slabs $ 12.00 per pound

Anything over the 4-inch bracket, please call or write for a quote for the service.
