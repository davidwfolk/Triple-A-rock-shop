PRIDAY RANCH COLLECTION


What you are looking at is 45 years of collecting Priday Ranch Plume slabs, heels and eggs which is the last collection I have to offer in selling out. Several of these slabs and heels were purchased back when you could buy a good slab or heel for less than $5.00 some 40 years ago and now finished cabs are selling in the area of $8.00 - $12.00 a carat, or higher, at any rock show. Now the plume bed has been dug out for a few years and the Richardson’s Ranch, Johnny and son Casey, of the Richardson’s Ranch Rock Shop only opens it during the Madras Rock Show in June when and if they feel like it. Not many eggs are taken out and in 2016 there was less than 50 pounds dug out all day by some 25 people. The rule of thumb is that you will cut and slab some 100 eggs to get just one egg that shows any plume. In this collection there is a total of fourteen (14) plastic shoe box trays in all and a combined weight of 134 pounds or approximately 1,700 pieces. It was at the time of the 2017 Madras Rock Show this collection was offered to my friends Johnny and Casey to see if they would buy the collection. Both said that if it wasn’t for them putting money in a new cutting house in Madagascar they would have bought it without question because of the amount and quality of material offered and price. Both said that the collection was worth between $170,000.00 to $175,000.00 and that my asking price of $17,500.00 was not only a deal but an absolute ridiculous steal! So, I’m selling all at one time for the amount of $17,500.00 and is a first come first serve sale and no splitting the collection or piecing it out in 1-2 slabs as price is firm for the entire collection. Shipping & insurance of this collection will be extra as there will be several large boxes filled with these items in bubble wrap sheets in order to ship or you can just stop by and load them up yourself if you want.
