Graveyard Point Agate Rough

Graveyard Point Agate Rough located on the Idaho side of Succor Creek(NW) near the town of Homedale is named after an 18th century settler massacre which occured in the vicinity. This material was available in such quantities as to promote an era of rockhounding in the Succor Creek/Owyhee Mountain locality.
